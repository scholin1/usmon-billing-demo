The idea in one minute

Your claims arrive ~95% prefilled — from schedulers, techs, and reading physicians — pulled straight from USMON. So the biller's job isn't data entry; it's confirming what's already there is correct.

AI does a first pass ahead of you and fills in the likely answers (the principal diagnosis from the reading report, the units, and so on). Every AI-filled field is highlighted so you always know what to double-check — you confirm or correct it, and it's logged. AI proposes; you decide.

There's no single "right" screen. The same claim and the same fields can be worked in the layout that suits you — a fast dense grid, a calm step-by-step flow, the familiar layout you know today, or an all-on-one-page cockpit. Pick your view.

Two roles, one system. Billers get a clean, stats-free screen built for verifying claim after claim. A Billing Supervisor gets one extra toggle that reveals team stats and a time-and-efficiency view (including a flag when a claim has been sitting idle). Billers never see it. Look for the Biller / Supervisor control in the top bar.
