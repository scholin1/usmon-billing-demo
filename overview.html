At a glance

What USMON does well today, and the automation coming next

A quick picture for a billing supervisor new to USMON. No jargon, no hard sell. Here is what the system already gets right, and the direction we are building toward.

Strong today

What USMON already does well

💵

Out-of-network is our specialty

USMON recovers dollars most billers leave behind, through negotiation and arbitration on out-of-network claims. That is the core strength.

📊

Built for real volume

Tens of thousands of active claims per client, organized so the ones on a deadline rise to the top.

✅

Review, not re-key

Most of each claim arrives already filled in from schedulers, technologists, and the reading physician. The biller confirms rather than types.

🔄

The whole lifecycle

Intake, filing (electronic and paper), follow-up, collections, and arbitration, all in one system.

🎯

The money goes to the right place

The billing entity and payer rules that decide where payment is sent, refined over 18 years of real claims.

🔌

Works with your clearinghouse

USMON connects to whichever clearinghouse a client already uses. Nothing to switch.

Coming next

New automation and features

🔐

Prior authorization, handled

Automatically check whether a payer requires authorization, request and track it, pull the medical necessity from the report, and land the approved number right on the claim, with the auth expiry tracked so nothing lapses.

✨

An AI first pass you just confirm

AI fills in the likely answers ahead of the biller and highlights every field it touched, so you confirm instead of hunting. Less typing, fewer misses.

🧬

Coding assist

Reads the report and flags CPT codes that may have been missed, so billable work is not left off the claim.

🛡️

A smarter pre-submission check

Ranks what is missing, proposes the fix for each item, and catches the rejections a clearinghouse would bounce, before the claim ever goes out.

🧭

Pick your view

The same claim in the layout each biller prefers, including a familiar view close to today's screens, so no one is forced to relearn their day.

📈

Supervisor oversight

Time and efficiency per biller and a flag when a claim sits idle, on your screen as the supervisor, kept off the billers' clean workspace.

🧾

Payments that post themselves

Electronic remittances match line by line automatically. Only the discrepancies need a person to look.

⏰

No missed deadlines

Filing and arbitration clocks are tracked so a deadline is never missed quietly.

💬

Reports on request

Ask for what you want in plain language and get the report, instead of waiting on a fixed menu.

An honest note: the items above roll out in phases. Some are in build, some in design. We would rather show you the real direction than promise a date we have not earned.

The throughline: catch more, key less, deny less, and never leave an out-of-network dollar or a deadline on the table. USMON already does the hard part well; the new work is about making it faster, more accurate, and easier for a new biller to pick up on day one.
